# Enterprise Security Policy

## 1. Password Requirements
All employee passwords must be at least 14 characters long, containing a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters. Passwords must be changed every 90 days.

## 2. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
MFA is mandatory for all internal systems, including email, VPN, and the HR portal.

## 3. Incident Reporting
Any suspected security breach, phishing email, or lost device must be reported immediately to the IT Security Desk at security@enterprise.com or by calling ext. 5555. 

## 4. Bring Your Own Device (BYOD)
Personal devices used for work must have the company MDM (Mobile Device Management) profile installed. Rooted or jailbroken devices are strictly prohibited from accessing company networks.
