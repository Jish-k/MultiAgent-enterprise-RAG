# Company Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) - Client Onboarding

## Phase 1: Contract Signing
1. Sales team finalizes the agreement.
2. Legal team reviews and countersigns via DocuSign.
3. The signed contract is uploaded to the CRM under the client's profile.

## Phase 2: Kickoff Meeting
1. The Customer Success Manager (CSM) schedules a kickoff meeting within 3 business days of contract signing.
2. The CSM sends a pre-kickoff questionnaire to the client.
3. A shared Slack channel is created for real-time communication.

## Phase 3: Technical Integration
1. The Implementation Engineer generates API keys.
2. The client is granted access to the sandbox environment.
3. Once UAT (User Acceptance Testing) is passed, production credentials are provided.
