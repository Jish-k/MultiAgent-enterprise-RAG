# Employee Handbook

## Welcome to the Company!
We are thrilled to have you onboard. This handbook is your guide to our company culture, values, and day-to-day operations.

## Core Values
1. **Customer First**: Everything we do is driven by customer success.
2. **Transparency**: We communicate openly and honestly.
3. **Innovation**: We encourage taking calculated risks to drive progress.

## Office Hours & Facilities
Our primary office is open from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM on weekdays. The office provides free coffee, snacks, and a gym facility on the 3rd floor. Parking validation is available at the reception.
