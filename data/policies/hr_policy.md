# General HR Policy & Code of Conduct

## 1. Equal Opportunity Employment
We are an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, religion, color, national origin, gender, sexual orientation, age, marital status, or disability status.

## 2. Workplace Harassment
The company has a zero-tolerance policy towards workplace harassment, including sexual, verbal, and physical harassment. All incidents should be reported to hr-confidential@enterprise.com.

## 3. Remote Work Policy
Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 2 days a week, subject to manager approval. Core working hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in the employee's local time zone, during which they must be available for meetings.

## 4. Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted bi-annually in June and December. Promotions and salary adjustments are tied to the December review cycle.
