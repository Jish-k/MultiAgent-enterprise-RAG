# Corporate Travel Policy
Effective Date: March 15, 2025

## 1. Flights
All flights under 6 hours must be booked in Economy Class. Flights exceeding 6 hours are eligible for Business Class, subject to VP approval. All bookings must be made through the internal travel portal (Concur).

## 2. Accommodation
The maximum daily allowance for hotels is:
- Tier 1 Cities (New York, London, Tokyo, San Francisco): $350/night
- Tier 2 Cities: $200/night
- Other locations: $150/night

## 3. Per Diem (Meals and Incidentals)
Employees are granted a per diem of $75 per day for domestic travel and $100 per day for international travel. Receipts are not required for per diem claims unless exceeding the allotted amount due to client dinners.

## 4. Reimbursement Process
Expenses must be submitted within 30 days of returning from the trip. Late submissions will not be processed.
