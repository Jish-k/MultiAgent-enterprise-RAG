### Q2: What is the maternity leave policy?
**Category:** HR Policies

**Retrieved Sources:** Employee_Handbook.pdf, Leave_Policy.pdf

**LLM Answer:**
According to our company policies, the maternity leave policy is as follows:

- Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid Maternity Leave, in accordance with the Maternity Benefit Act.
- Maternity leave must be approved by the HR Head and the respective Department Head.
- The leave can be availed starting up to 8 weeks before the expected date of delivery.
- Employees returning from maternity leave may also request flexible working arrangements or extended remote work, subject to managerial approval.

Please note that this information is based on the provided company policies, specifically Document 1 (Leave_Policy.pdf, Page 7, 5. Maternity Leave) and Document 2 (Employee_Handbook.pdf, Page 13, 2. Leaves).

### Q7: Who approves business travel?
**Category:** Travel Policies

**Retrieved Sources:** Travel_Policy.pdf

**LLM Answer:**
Based on the provided company policies, the approval process for business travel varies depending on the type of travel. 

For domestic travel, approval is required from the immediate reporting manager. 

For international travel, approval must be granted by the respective Vice President and the CEO. 

Additionally, for flights exceeding 8 hours of continuous travel, VP approval is required, regardless of the type of travel.
